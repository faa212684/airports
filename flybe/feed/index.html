Flybe is a British airline based in Exeter, England. Until its sale to Connect Airways (January 2019), it was the largest independent regional airline in Europe. Flybe carries 8 million passengers a year between 81 airports across the UK and the rest of Europe, with over 210 routes across 15 countries. Its two hubs are Birmingham and Manchester airports but it also has a number of codeshares allowing connections to long-haul flights from airports such as London Heathrow, Paris CDG, Dublin and Amsterdam. The airline is a member of the European Regions Airline Association.

]]> Flybe Excess baggage

Flybe offers the opportunity to pre-book and increase your hold luggage allowance up to 40kg

To ensure that no excess baggage charges are levied, please ensure that you:

  • Select the hold luggage allowance pre-booking option or ticket type to suit your travel needs
  • Benefit from pre-booking your hold luggage in advance
  • If carrying exceptional items of baggage, that these are within the permitted allowance
  • Ensure your hold luggage and cabin bag items’ size and weight are within your selected allowances prior to travel

Excess baggage for Just Fly customers

As a part of your Just Fly ticket, a combined 10kg cabin bag allowance applies, including the additional under the seat cabin baggage; furthermore you are able to pre-book a selection of 15kg, 20kg, 23kg or 40kg hold luggage to suit your travel needs.

Excess baggage for Get More customers

As a part of your Get More ticket, a combined 10kg cabin bag allowance applies, including the additional under the seat cabin baggage; furthermore you are able to increase your standard 20kg baggage allowance to either 23kg or 40kg hold luggage to suit your needs to travel.

Excess baggage for All In customers

As a part of your All In ticket, a combined 10kg cabin bag allowance applies, including the additional under the seat cabin baggage. Furthermore your hold luggage allowance is comprised  of two items of hold luggage up to a combined weight of 40kg.
